Big Dog Adventures: A Hardcoded Java RPG

Last Modified: 10/13/2024

"Big Dog Adventures" was a year long project done the senior year of my bachelors degree. It was co-developed with Dylan Gibbs and Jason Strack. This top-down rpg includes 6000+ lines of code, and features such as:
  • Controllable character
  • Combat
  • Collision Ddtection
  • Game saving
  • Enemy tracking
  • Dynamic sound
  • Eight unique maps
  • Equipable Objects for character and combat customization
